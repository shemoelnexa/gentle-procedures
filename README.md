# Gentle Procedures Dubai — Homepage Concepts

Two homepage concept directions for review. Per the signed brief, both share **one design** and differ **only in the hero treatment** ("one site, two front doors"):

- **`concept-a.html` — "The Threshold"** · full-bleed cinematic hero (dark, image-led).
- **`concept-b.html` — "Trust in Silence"** · full-bleed soft-light hero, centred (light, serene).
- **`index.html`** · gallery linking both.

Everything below the hero is identical across the two concepts.

## Status
Shown in **State One** (pre-launch) form. Fonts (Fraunces / Inter) and photography are **interim, licence-clean stand-ins** for the production faces (Canela / Söhne) and the launch-film stills. Credential figures (e.g. `[200,000]`, DHA licence) render as editable placeholder tokens.
